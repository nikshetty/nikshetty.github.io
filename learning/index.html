My Learning Path

Given that there is no set road map to learning Data Science, most aspiring data scientists have to find their own learning paths. I too did my own research trying to figure out the wide variety of required skills. I tried a few popular data science related MOOC courses initially. These did not turn out to be a good fit for me since most of them were somewhat theoretical, required watching long videos with not enough hands-on. I eventually discovered Dataquest.io, tried some of their free courses initially and got hooked on their content and format. I really got going on my data science learning journey after subscribing to their monthly subscription and committing to Dataquest's Data Scientist Path. The two main reasons I recommend Dataquest is their well laid out road map of courses which cover the gamut of data science skills and their Codecademy-like interface and teaching platform involving a ton of challeninging hands-on projects. Also, since I already had prior work experience with SQL and Python, I was able to breeze through those courses.

Listed below are the courses that I completed as part of Dataquest's Data Scientist path. I also completed the highly recommended Andrew Ng's Machine Learning course in Coursera in parallel. The links open to certificates for the completed course. Needless to say there is always more to learn and I will be adding more to this list.
